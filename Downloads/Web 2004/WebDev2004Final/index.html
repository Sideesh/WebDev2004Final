Welcome

I grew up lighting diyas on Diwali, throwing colors on Holi, and hearing stories of Ganesha and Krishna. But when friends asked, "What is Hinduism really about?" — I realized it's not easy to explain.

This site is my attempt to make it simple, respectful, and approachable. No preaching. Just clarity.

Key Ideas

  • Dharma: Living with duty, righteousness, and moral order.
  • Karma: Actions shape your future — good deeds, good results.
  • Moksha: Liberation from the cycle of birth and rebirth.
  • Yoga: Path to union with the divine — not just poses.
  • Ahimsa: Non-violence in thought, word, and action.
